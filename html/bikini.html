Enter the universe of SpongeBob and his friends. This is Bikini Bottom City. It's a fictional city, of course, but nonetheless an amazing map to explore. It includes most of the locations as seen in the animated TV series. Some of the houses include SpongeBob's pineapple house and along with the neighbouring houses. There's is also the Bikini Bottom Mall and lots of other places to explore we can assure you.

n

The map is ported from the PC version of Minecraft. That's why some of the areas in the map are black and looks bugged. Ignore that if you can or replace them with new blocks. When we tested the map it was completely free of lag and never crashed.
