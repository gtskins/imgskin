Is this multiplayer compatible? 

No, this map is intended for single-player play. If you try this with other people game-breaking bugs or soft locks may appear. If you want to play Imperial Rain with friends there's information on it in the spawn hub.

How do I play or process?

You first spawn in a hub, it has information on how to play and what to do.

To process the story you go to the white beacon and talk to the NPC to begin the mission.

The Story

The game takes place in the year 3 ABY or during The Empire Strikes Back! You play as Miller Clark, he's freshly out of the Empire after being conscripted. This is one of the first open-world maps on Minecraft Bedrock to have a protagonist. This map also features voice acting and cutscenes! Miller Clark is looking for a new line of work and not interested in the traditional ways of work. Rinson, an old friend of Miller is eagerly waiting for him to come back so they can start their "work". See Star Wars never seen before in this Open World game where you take in the eyes of Miller and his criminal experience across the galaxy! It takes on a more mature style on Star Wars!

The Maps

There are three open planets seen in the films!

Coruscant

Tatooine

Bespin

They also have iconic locations seen in the films!

 

Content

There are many blasters you can purchase in-game that are seen in the films. There is food that you can eat that is seen in films and unique enemies! You can freely roam the planets.
